Lemon Drizzle Cake

Description

Lemon drizzle cake is one of the nicest cakes to make, a soft sponge with a sharp touch of lemon.

Ingredients

  • Cake

    • 200g Self Raising Flour
    • 225g Butter
    • 225g Caster Sugar
    • Zest of 3 Lemons
    • 4 Eggs
    • 1 tsp Baking Powder
    • 50g Ground Almonds
    • Juice 1 1/2 Lemons

  • Lemon Icing

    • Zest of 1 Lemon
    • Juice 1/2 Lemon
    • Icing Sugar

Steps

  1. Preheat fan oven to 320°F/160°C.
  2. Lightly grease and line a 20cm cake tin.
  3. Beat the butter, caster sugar and lemon zest together in a large bowl until its a pale cream.
  4. Whisk in the eggs one at a time.
  5. Fold in the baking powder,ground almonds and self raising flour.
  6. Whisk in the lemon juice .
  7. Pour mix into the lined cake tin and cover with foil
  8. Place in the oven and bake for 30 minutes.
  9. Remove the foil and bake for a further 25 mins
  10. Remove from the oven place on a wire rack and allow the cake to cool in the tin for 10 minutes
  11. While the cake is cooling, mix the lemon juice, zest and just enough icing sugar to make runny icing
  12. Pour the runny icing over the cake and allow to cool for a further 30 mins in the tin
  13. Remove from the tin place on the wire rack and allow to completely cool before serving
