Gingerbread

Description

This gingerbread is a quick and simple yet delicious tray bake, which gives that warm ginger kick for those shortening autumnal days.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/4 lbs Self Raising Flour
  • 6 oz Margarine
  • 4 oz Caster Sugar
  • 3 tsp Ground Ginger
  • 8 oz Golden Syrup
  • 3 oz Currants
  • 3 oz Sultanas
  • 2 oz Candid Peel

Steps

  1. Preheat fan oven to 300°F/150°C.
  2. Lightly grease a baking tray.
  3. Melt the golden syrup and margarine in a pan, and allow to cool so as not to burn you when completeing the next step.
  4. Place the rest of the ingredients in a large bowl and pour over the melted golden syrup and margarine.
  5. Mix with a wooden spoon to start, once the mix has come together give a final mix with clean hands.
  6. Put the mix into the tray and press down into all corners smooth with the side of a table knife.
  7. Place in the oven and bake for 30/45minutes.
  8. Allow to cool slightly, then cut into 1 inch wide fingers(across the width of the tray) while still warm.
