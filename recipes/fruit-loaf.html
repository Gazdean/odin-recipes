Fruit Loaf

Description

The fruit loaf all dads will love, great on its own or slightly buttered with a nice cup of tea.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb Self Raising Flour
  • 4 oz Margarine
  • 8 oz Caster Sugar
  • 8 oz Sultanas
  • 8 floz Milk
  • 1 Egg

Steps

  1. Preheat fan oven to 325°F/170°C.
  2. Lightly grease a large loaf tin then line the base and sides with parchment paper. Lightly butter the parchment.
  3. Melt the margarine, milk and sultanas in a pan nearly to boiling point, and allow to cool slightly.
  4. Place the rest of the ingredients in a large bowl and pour over the melted margarine, milk and sultanas.
  5. Mix with a wooden spoon to combine well.
  6. Pour the mix into the prelined loaf tin
  7. Place in the oven and bake for 50/60 minutes
